Carter Stephens is a co-founder of the NAI Ohio Equities investment sales team. He advises buyers and sellers in a multitude of different asset classes to maximize their holdings. This is accomplished by providing unmatched market knowledge, financial analysis, and service.

Prior to his career in commercial real estate, Carter was a financial advisor in the capital market space where his team managed over $100,000,000 of assets. His unique knowledge of different market sectors allows Carter to advise and deliver for his clients in a fiduciary capacity.

Why did you decide to get into Commercial Real Estate?

I have always found commercial real estate to be the most unique asset class. It is something you can see, feel, touch, and change. I enjoy building relationships with property owners and investors to help them get a wholistic understanding of their asset and determine whether there is a better alternative in the marketplace.

What has been the best part of Columbus's growth over the past few decades?

The best part for me has been seeing the tangible transformation Columbus has gone through. You not only feel the growth but actually see it through new development all around the city. I think there is tremendous opportunity for continued growth and look forward to playing an intricate part in that.

Why did you decide to make Columbus your home?

I grew up on the east coast. Upon graduating from Ohio University, I had to decide whether I should go back home or stay close to a place I grew fondly of. I had a great support system from school who lived in Columbus and I decided to put down roots in the state capital. Eight years later, I can confidently say I made the right decision. Columbus has so much to offer, and I am happy I found a city that matches my personality.
