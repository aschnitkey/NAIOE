Philip Bird, Vice President of Office Brokerage at NAI Ohio Equities, specializes in the leasing and sale of general and medical office properties. Philip has been an agent at NAI Ohio Equities since 2006, but since joining the NAI Office Brokerage team in 2016, he has completed over 210 transactions totaling 2,500,000 square feet and $250 million.

Philip brings local market expertise and personal service, backed by the resources of NAI Global, to all of his real estate clients. Believing that honesty and integrity are the foundation of relationships, he always works in his clients’ best interests to deliver solutions and options that meet clients’ real estate needs.

Philip is a graduate of the Fisher College of Business at The Ohio State University with a BS in Business Administration.

Why did you decide to get into Commercial Real Estate?

I became very interested in real estate during college while leasing/managing apartments near campus. After speaking with contacts in the business I was intrigued by the idea of beginning a career in an industry that is exciting, constantly evolving, and has essentially no limit on growth potential.

Why did you decide to make Columbus your home?

I initially moved to Columbus to attend The Ohio State University. It didn’t take long to realize how special this place is. Between the people and the city itself I knew I wanted to put down roots here.

Where do you like to vacation?

The Caribbean and other warm/tropical climates to take a break from Ohio winters. Also Colorado to visit family and enjoy the mountains.

What is your favorite hidden gem in Columbus?

The Pub Out Back – Old Worthington.
