Ryan specializes in the leasing and sale of industrial & office properties. With Ryan being a lifelong resident of Columbus, Ohio, he uses his market knowledge to provide his clients with all-inclusive representation. Ryan is upfront and honest which helps him maintain long-lasting relationships with his clients.

Ryan understands the importance of hard-work, dedication and integrity in establishing a strong rapport with his clients, whether they are investors, owners or tenants.

Ryan grew up in Upper Arlington, Ohio, and attended Upper Arlington High School. After high school, Ryan continued his education at the University of Dayton where he received a Bachelor’s Degree in Communications Management. Ryan is very hands on in his community and currently resides in Upper Arlington.

Commercial Real Estate Services
  • Executes leases for office, retail, and industrial properties in dual and single broker relationships.
  • Assists businesses, landlords, tenants, and investors in buying, selling, and leasing real estate.
  • Performs door-to-door marketing to landlords and business owners to solicit selling, buying, and leasing services.
  • Negotiates lease terms between landlords and tenants to execution.
  • Supervises construction phase of tenant improvements.
  • Prepares and submits proposals for future or existing clients.
