Joe is a Certified Commercial Investment Member designee. CCIM designees are recognized as leading experts in commercial investment real estate.

Joe is responsible for handling all aspects of buying and selling and/or leasing commercial real estate for a wide range of clients. He specializes in buyer and tenant representation, investment sales, land sales and acquisition and office and industrial sales and leasing. Joe's approach to real estate is centered on forming long-lasting partnerships with clients in order to achieve their real estate goals.

Joe previously worked as a Certified Real Estate Appraiser within the Central Ohio Market, which gives Joe a unique analytical approach to evaluating real estate.

Click here to view Joe Menninger's website

Why did you decide to make Commercial Real Estate your career?

Not many professions require you to be creative and analytical while at the same time have business development skills. Commercial real estate brokerage is a challenging career, but at the same time very rewarding. Bringing a deal to completion is a heck of a lot of fun.

Describe a memorable transaction:

My most memorable, and unique transaction was early in my career and involved an actual swap of two properties in different states. A client of mine traded a commercial warehouse in Columbus for a very nice beach vacation home in South Carolina. It was a complicated deal and during the contingency period, a hurricane went through and damaged the house, further complicating the deal. We were able to get it done, but took some creative thinking.

Why did you decide to make Columbus your home?

Columbus is a great city. I grew up in Cincinnati and made the trip up I-71 in 1996 to attend OSU and have never left. Columbus has a small town feel for a good sized city. I like that.

What’s a favorite pastime you enjoy?

I enjoy going out and exploring the city. Whether it’s taking my daughter to COSI or the zoo, checking out a new (or going back to a favorite) restaurant, or riding the many bike paths around the city, getting out and enjoying Columbus is always a lot of fun.
