Joe is a Certified Commercial Investment Member, which is widely recognized as signifying the leading experts in commercial investment real estate. Previously he worked as a Certified Real Estate Appraiser within the Central Ohio Market, which gives Joe a unique analytical approach to evaluating real estate.

Joe is responsible for handling all aspects of buying, selling and/or leasing commercial real estate for a diverse range of clients. He specializes in buyer and tenant representation, the sale, lease or acquisition of industrial and office space, investment properties as well as undeveloped land deals. Joe's approach to real estate is centered on forming long-lasting partnerships with clients to achieve their business and real estate goals.

Why did you decide to make Commercial Real Estate your career?

Commercial real estate brokerage is an ever-changing and challenging industry, which drew me in immediately because I love a challenge. The ultimate reward for me is the satisfaction of executing a deal with a strategic approach while fostering key relationships in our community. Few professions allow you the latitude to stretch your creativity while also analyzing things from a financial growth perspective. Not to mention, the journey it takes to get a deal to completion is a heck of a lot of fun.

Describe a memorable transaction:

My most unique and memorable transaction was early on in my career which involved an actual swap of two properties in different states. My client was looking to trade a commercial warehouse in Columbus for a vacation beach home in South Carolina. From the start it was a complicated deal, then during the contingency period, a hurricane went through the coastline. This resulted in damage to the house, which added a new layer of difficulty. Thankfully with a bit of creative thinking, we were able to get to the finish line.

Why did you decide to make Columbus your home?

I grew up in Cincinnati and made the trip up I-71 in 1996 to attend OSU and have never left. I quickly saw that Columbus is a fantastic city! It's rapidly growing with a busy urban center but still maintains that small-town feel. I like that.

What’s a favorite pastime you enjoy?

I enjoy going out and exploring the city, whether it’s taking my daughter to COSI or the Columbus zoo, checking out a new or revisiting an old favorite restaurant, and enjoying the fresh air riding the many bike paths around the city. Getting out and enjoying Columbus is always a lot of fun.
