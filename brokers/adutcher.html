Andy Dutcher is Senior Vice President at NAI Ohio Equities. As the co-founder of The Office Brokerage Group, his responsibilities for his clients include developing strategy and implementation of the leasing, sale and investment of commercial real estate. In addition to this, Andy is also responsible for long term business development efforts within the company. Andy achieved his SIOR designation, which represents today’s most knowledgeable, experienced and successful commercial real estate brokerage specialists.

Buyers, sellers, tenants, and landlords all trust Andy to provide comprehensive and unique solutions to meet their goals. With his strong background in market conditions, site selection, and deal negotiation, his clients trust that they have all the information they need to make highly informed real estate decisions.

Andy’s success in the Columbus downtown and suburban office markets have made him an NAI Ohio Equities Top Producer every year since 2013, a multi-year recipient of the CoStar Power Broker award, Columbus Business First “People to Know in Real Estate” in 2017, Columbus Realtors Top 10 Under 40 in 2020, and Columbus Realtors Top Office Producer in 2021. Andy is a graduate of Ohio University.

Why did you decide to get into Commercial Real Estate?

After working on Capitol Hill in Washington D.C. for two years after college, I found myself wanting to make a career change. One of my best friends suggested I should consider commercial real estate so in 2007 I got licensed and then the big recession hit. Although times were tough, it created a solid foundation for how I run my real estate practice and treat my clients with honesty, integrity and respect.

Where do you like to vacation?

Marco Island, FL and any of the Great Lakes regions. I prefer laid back and relaxing destinations where I can unplug and spend quality time with the family.

What is your favorite hidden gem in Columbus?

Hellas Carryout in Shawnee Hills has been one of my favorite places to eat since I was in elementary school. They have the best omelettes and gyros in town.
