  • Bio

As Property Manager, Chris Harbert possesses nearly 25 years of experience in hospitality and facilities management. Currently, Chris manages nearly 1.6 million square feet of commercial properties for clients such as Mt. Carmel, Ohio ENT and Allergy, Ohio University Medical College, Columbus School for Girls, Ohio Health and Daimler.

While at NAI Ohio Equities, Chris earned his real estate license and joined the Building Owners and Managers Association (BOMA). He continues to take great pride in ensuring that any issues that arise are managed appropriately with great consideration and professionalism.

Chris enjoys spending time with his family where together they explore different festivals across the state. He also enjoys discovering small breweries and working tirelessly at having the best yard on the block.
