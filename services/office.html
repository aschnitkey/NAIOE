NAI Ohio Equities has decades of experience within the Central Ohio office market. We have an understanding of the current market and how it is evolving, including recent trends. Our commercial office brokers use innovative techniques while guiding you through all the details of the transaction, including market comps, negotiations, and the construction process. Whether you are looking to buy or sell, fill vacant space, or relocate your business, our brokers will find a solution that works for you.

4th Quarter 2021

  • For the second consecutive quarter the Columbus office market experienced positive net absorption, which totaled 270,000 SF in Q4 21. Average lease rates held at just over $21/SF, and new development remains steady with nearly 1.5 million SF under construction.
  • “Despite the lingering pandemic, leasing activity continues to strengthen with more tenants in the market and larger transactions occurring in both the suburbs and downtown. We anticipate this trend will continue in 2022, especially with the demand for high quality and new construction opportunities that are well amenitized.” - Philip Bird, VP Office Brokerage
