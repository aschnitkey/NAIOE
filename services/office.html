NAI Ohio Equities has decades of experience within the Central Ohio office market. We have an understanding of the current market and how it is evolving, including recent trends. Our commercial office brokers use innovative techniques while guiding you through all the details of the transaction, including market comps, negotiations, and the construction process. Whether you are looking to buy or sell, fill vacant space, or relocate your business, our brokers will find a solution that works for you.
