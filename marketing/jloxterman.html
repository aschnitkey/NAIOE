  • Bio

For more than a decade, Jenna Loxterman has been a vital member of one of NAI Ohio Equities’ most successful teams, the Office Brokerage Group. She supports the team’s marketing, business development, and transaction management initiatives—efforts that have contributed to doubling its production over the same period.

Now as Senior Marketing Coordinator, Jenna collaborates closely with marketing leadership to execute campaigns that align with company goals. She also leads the Marketing Assistant team, overseeing project execution, streamlining workflows, and driving marketing performance.

Jenna is actively involved in philanthropic efforts, including serving on the Board of the Furniture Bank of Central Ohio. She is a member of the National Association of REALTORS®, the Ohio Association of REALTORS®, and Columbus REALTORS®. She holds a B.A. in Marketing from the University of Mount Union and an M.S. in Marketing and Communications from Franklin University.

In her free time, Jenna enjoys reading, binge-watching streaming series, and spending time with her husband and two daughters.
