Multifamily Opinion of Value Analysis

NAI Ohio Equities comprehends the importance of providing Multifamily property owners the information they need to understand their assets value in today’s every changing market conditions.

Because of that, we offer a free of charge “Opinion of Value” which will include:

Investment Highlights & Objectives

Economic Trends in the Market & Submarket

Multifamily Sale & Rental Comps

Financial Analysis of the asset

By providing Multifamily property owners with this analysis, they can get a wholelistic understanding of their property and how it is positioned within the overall market. This allows owners to:

  • Achieve the highest possible Internal Rate of Return
  • Capitalize on Market Timing
  • Remain aware of Net Worth
  • Constantly Gauge Investment Returns
  • Identify Value Add Opportunities
